Chess Engine
A simple chess engine running on a minimax decision tree, thinking six moves deep and using alpha-beta pruning to skip analyzing moves that aren't valuable. The engine assesses key opening strategies, calculates allowed moves, assigns values to pieces and positions, tracks move history, and evaluates both sides' positions as the game progresses. For ease of play, pawns are automatically promoted to queens. The engine presents a fair challenge to an average player. It has an estimated ELO of 1300-1400.
