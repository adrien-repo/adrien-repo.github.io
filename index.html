Chess Engine
A simple chess engine running on a minimax decision tree. It goes three moves deep and uses alpha-beta pruning. The engine assigns values to pieces and auto-promotes pawns to queens for ease. It shows move history and evaluates both sides' positions as the game progresses. The engine has an estimated ELO of 1300. It presents a fair challenge, especially in the midgame, though it sometimes falters in the endgame.
